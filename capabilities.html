Why KDL Supply Management is Important

KDL Supply Management is crucial for any organization because doing it well can introduce several benefits to your organization. From freight to packaging, to fulfillment, to small parcel services, KDL is driven to reimagine the entire supply chain. We provide a seamless experience for retailers with our proprietary systems and business intelligence.

Finished goods are no longer 100 percent made by brand names. Now, parts and materials are sourced from outside vendors who provide them across a global supply chain. According to an infographic from the National Institute of Standards and Technologyâ€™s Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership, 230,000 small manufacturers contribute to US supply chains and 42 percent of all US manufacturing workers are employed by small manufacturers. This creates an ever-growing, complex web of materials providers, suppliers, distributors, logistics companies and manufacturers. Our Capabilities

Kitting and Assembly

Times have changed and you have the idea to create wellness packages for all your employees in your company. You want to show that you are not alone and you are in this together. You've got all the pieces, let us put it all together! No matter the size of your project, KDL has an experienced team and the tools to assemble your project efficiently and effectively!

Shipping and Receiving

You have an order that needs to be packed, wrapped, and loaded for transport. At KDL Services, we understand the components and care that go into fulfilling orders and getting them ready and loaded for transport. By receiving your projects for your order, we will pack, wrap, and load orders that can not be managed at your facility.

Contract Warehousing

As we are a kitting and packing company, you can store your upcoming projects and order materials within our warehouse. This means you will have access to your materials to fulfill your next order whenever necessary. At KDL we understand the high demand for a product and limited space for housing. We use a state-of-the-art organization system that allows us to pick and load your product with easy access, to send to you.

Printing Services

KDL has the ability to handle all of your print and mail projects, ranging from a short-run quick turn flyer or brochure to a complex, multi-touch direct mail program. Our team of seasoned professionals will work to gain a thorough knowledge of your requirements and implement the right solution for you. We have strategic partnerships with both local and national suppliers across all segments of the print industry. These partnerships allow us to find the right solution for all of your needs. Our range of products includes pamphlets, brochures, pocket folders, booklets, folding cartons, and much more.
